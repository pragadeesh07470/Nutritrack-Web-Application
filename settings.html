NutriTrack
Version 1.0.0 — Free Edition

NutriTrack is a privacy-first nutrition tracking app. All your data lives in your browser's local storage — nothing is ever sent to a server.

📋 Changelog
v1.0.0 Initial release — Dashboard, Food Log (18 foods), BMI Calculator, Macro Calculator, Download Reports, Profile, and Settings.
v0.9.0 Beta — Calorie ring, macro progress bars, weight history chart, and streak tracking added.
v0.8.0 Alpha — Core food logging and localStorage persistence established.
